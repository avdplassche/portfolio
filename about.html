About Me

I'm Alexandre, a dedicated Computer Science student passionate about low-level programming and system efficiency. I thrive on tackling complex challenges, especially those involving building tools from the ground up, like creating custom shells or developing high-performance simulation engines.

My work focuses heavily on C and C++, where I explore deep concepts in **parsing, memory management, concurrent processing, and data-oriented design**. I believe that understanding the inner workings of systems is key to developing robust and efficient software.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy learning about electronics and experimenting with hardware, which often inspires new software projects. This portfolio showcases my journey and commitment to continuous learning in the world of computer science.
